Evaluation

MIR-1K dataset + BSS-EVAL metrics

Dataset

  • MIR-1K dataset
  • 4 to 13 sec
  • Extracted from 110 Chinese karaoke pop songs
  • (sample)
  • The singing voice and the music were mixed at -5, 0, 5 dB SNRs, respectively
    (i.e. the mixture with 5 dB SNR has the most louder voice)

BSS-EVAL metrics

$$ SIR := 10 \log_{10}{\frac{||s_{target}||^2}{||e_{interf}||^2}} $$
$$ SAR := 10 \log_{10}{\frac{||s_{target} + e_{interf} + e_{noise}||^2}{||e_{artif}||^2}} $$
$$ SDR := 10 \log_{10}{\frac{||s_{target}||^2}{||e_{interf} + e_{noise} + e_{artif}||^2}} $$
